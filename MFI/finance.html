Interest rates for different amounts

  • For ₹1000: Interest is ₹5 per day
  • For ₹2000: Interest is ₹10 per day
  • For ₹3000: Interest is ₹15 per day
  • For ₹4000: Interest is ₹20 per day
  • For ₹5000: Interest is ₹25 per day

If the amount falls between the listed values, the interest rate will be interpolated accordingly. For example:

  • For an amount between ₹1000 and ₹2000, the interest rate will be between ₹5 and ₹10.
  • For an amount between ₹2000 and ₹3000, the interest rate will be between ₹10 and ₹15.
  • For an amount between ₹3000 and ₹4000, the interest rate will be between ₹15 and ₹20.
  • For an amount between ₹4000 and ₹5000, the interest rate will be between ₹20 and ₹25.
NOTE: Don't forget to pay on the return date. If any delay's inform us before the date. If delay has taken place extra charges will be added to your interest.
