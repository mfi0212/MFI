I'm late

If you are late in returning the amount, the interest will keep adding to the amount.

To avoid complications, keep the following in mind:

  • You must pay before the return date to avoid additional charges.
  • You may get the amount on the borrowed date, but the return must be completed before return date or on return date before 10 AM; otherwise, it will be considered late.
  • Late payments can quickly increases.
  • Contact us if you think you'll be late so we can help you.

Verify your total return amount in your account details before returning the amount.
